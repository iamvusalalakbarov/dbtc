About

DBTC stands for "Don't Break The Chain" which is a famous methodology created by Jerry Seinfeld. He used this methodology to improve his skills about comedy, but it is suitable for everyone who needs to get a progress on anything; it can be reading a book, doing some sport or famously — learning programming.

The methodology is simple: You decide for your goal, get a calendar and everday after doing your goal put a cross on the respective day of calendar. Then your new goal is not to break the chain formed by crosses.

As a developer of this project, I learned this methodology from Turkish youtuber Barış Özcan. He prepares videos on this concept at every New Year's Eve and encourages his subscribers to define New Year goals. I learned lots of my programming skills with the help of this methodology by applying it to my daily life. Then I decided to create a website on this topic by using the skills I learned.

Nowadays, everything becomes virtual; so I thought it is also a need to apply this methodology not on a paper, on a web app instead. As foreigner students like me, it is really hard to keep those things on a paper and carry it with you without hurting or damaging it. That's the real reason behind the project idea.
